Xinqi Liu | 刘 鑫 琦

I am currently an Assistant Professor at School of Robotics and National Engineering Research Center of Robot Visual Perception and Control Technology, Hunan University (HNU) . Previously, I worked at Department of Computer Vision Technology (VIS), Baidu Inc. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from School of Mechanical Engineering, Zhejiang University , advised by Prof. Jituo Li.

My research focuses on 3D digital humans, 3D vision, and embodied intelligence.

We are looking for students and research assistants. Please do not hesitate to email us if you are interested in working with us.
